Rekayasa Perangkat Lunak (RPL), or Software Engineering or SE) is a professional field that studies software development methods including creation, maintenance, organizational management of software development and quality management. Software engineering is changing the software itself to develop, maintain, and rebuild it using engineering principles to produce software that can work more efficiently and effectively for users.
